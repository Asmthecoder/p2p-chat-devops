Evaluation Appraisal
====================
Scalability latency slope (p95 per peer): -0.001 ms/peer
Scalability throughput slope: -3.900 msg/s per peer
Reliability success-rate drop (0%->max loss): 23.33%

Latency scaling: Strong. Tail latency grows slowly as peers increase.
Throughput scaling: Degrading with scale; investigate contention and retries.
Reliability: Acceptable with degradation; retries/backoff are recommended.

Design trade-offs observed:
- Lower timeout improves responsiveness but may reduce success rate under jitter.
- Aggressive retransmission can improve delivery but may reduce throughput at scale.
- Adding integrity checks increases CPU overhead slightly while improving trustworthiness.
